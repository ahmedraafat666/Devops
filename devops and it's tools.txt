task 1 : searching in dev ops vs Agile in progress

devops : is a software development (development operation) method which focuses on communication, integration, and collaboration among IT professionals to enables rapid deployment of products

while Agile : Agile refers to an iterative approach which focuses on collaboration, customer feedback, and small, rapid releases.
